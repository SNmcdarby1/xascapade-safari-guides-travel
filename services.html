Tour Infomation

Requirements

  • A Valid passport that is six month from the date of entry with at least two blank pages to travel

  • A Kenyan entry visa
  • A valid access point ticket either by Air, Road, Water, Rail
  • A confirmed Bookings ( safari booking, Park fee tickets )
  • Accommodation if you are staying over
  • I would recommend Anti Malaria tablets
  • And mosquito repellent

    • Included

  • A full National tours package includes (3 meals a day: lunch, dinner & breakfast), please note that this only applys if accommodation is included in the package.
  • A recommended National park of your desire.
  • A 4X4 private tour with a pop-up roof for good viewing and photographing together with a cool box to cool your drinks.
  • Professional English speaking tour guide/driver.
  • Park entrance fees for our vehicle and driver /tour guide is required.
  • For any special need please contact us dirrectly before booking your safari

    • KWS services

  • Respect the privacy of the wildlife, this is their habitat.
  • Beware of the animals, they are wild and can be unpredictable.
  • Don't crowd the animals or make sudden noises or movements.
  • Do not feed the animals, it upsets their diet and leads to human dependence.
  • Please keep quiet, noise disturbs the wildlife.
  • Stay in your vehicle at all times, except at designated picnic or walking areas, consult your driver/tour guide.
